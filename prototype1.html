Prototype 1 - Number of Incidents reported in Each Batallion for the Year 2019

Aryan Dilip Choudhary

Wrangling specific to prototype

For this tableau prototype the data is filtered by "Batallion" not "B99", "Year" is 2019. The data is grouped by Batallion, Watch Date (Months). The data is aggregated by Incident Number as sum.

    Filtered By: Batallion not "B99", Year 2019
    Grouping By: Batallion, and Watch Date (Months)
    Aggregated By: Incident Number (sum)

Tableau Prototype

Conclusion based on the prototype

For year 2019, "B02" is the most active Batallion. December 2019 is the month with the highest number of incidents for the Batallion "B02". The second most active Batallion is "B03". The prototype shows an increase in rate of incidents from January to December except for the month of July and November.

Planned Interactivity

I have planned for updating the d3 visualizations with the following interactivities:

  • Highlight all the bars related to a particular Batallion if one of the bars of that particular Batallion is hovered on i.e. Face all of the non-hovered bars belonging to the other Batallions.
  • Providing a toggle for ordering the data by Batallion for the Year 2019
  • Hovering over a bar will show a pie chart showing the number of incidents based on call type group (Alarm, Fire, Non-life Threatning, and Life Threatning).
  • on demand data display i.e. when a user hovers on a circle then a tool tip pops up with specific details of the data
